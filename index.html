UK Property Stats

House Prices and sales have become a national obsession over the last few years. This website gives you an overview into the numbers of house sales over the last year, and the prices at which they have been sold. Look at the map of the UK to find an overview of the sales and prices in different locations. Then view the sales and price charts below.

About

This UK Property site shows the latest prices and number of sales in cities around the UK. The statistics can also be divided for different house types.

Numbers change over time and the line graphs show the changes that have occured over time in the number of sales and prices. Whether this is a good indication of whats going to happen into the future remains to be seen, so be sure to follow the latest updates.
